Telephone Number Validator App

In the US, phone numbers can be formatted in many ways. Here are some examples of valid formats for US phone numbers:

1 555-555-5555
1 (555) 555-5555
1(555)555-5555
1 555 555 5555
5555555555
555-555-5555
(555)555-5555

This app checks if a number is a valid United States phone number.
